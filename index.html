Get To Know Us



Miss Ecosystem International (MEI) is a pioneering global pageant created to empower young women as environmental leaders and advocates for sustainable terrestrial ecosystems. Unlike traditional pageants, MEI transcends beauty—focusing on intelligence, advocacy, and tangible real-world environmental impact, fully aligned with the United Nations 2030 Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). Contestants will showcase innovative projects in areas such as green energy, sustainable agriculture, wildlife conservation, and climate action. The maiden edition, themed “The Land is Green,” will take place in May 2026 in Malaysia . The event expects participation from over 300 contestants worldwide, culminating in a spectacular live finale celebrating sustainability, cultural diversity, and youth innovation. Partnering with MEI offers Samdan Group an unparalleled opportunity to: Position itself as a global sustainability leader. Engage directly with environmentally conscious young audiences. Enhance brand equity through alignment with impactful social change. We warmly invite Samdan Group to join us on this transformative journey.
