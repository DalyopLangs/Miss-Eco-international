About Us


Miss Ecosystem International is a dedicated group of environmental enthusiasts passionate about promoting sustainability and eco-awareness in Nigeria. Our mission is to educate, inspire, and empower individuals to take action in protecting our ecosystem and mitigating the impact of human activities on the environment. Our Focus Areas We concentrate on Climate Change Mitigation, Sustainable Waste Management, Conservation of Biodiversity, and Environmental Education, working to address the pressing issues affecting our planet. Our Initiatives We organize Community Clean-up Events, host Environmental Workshops and Seminars, run Advocacy Campaigns, and collaborate with Local Organizations to drive change.
